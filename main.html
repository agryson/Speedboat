Speedboat for Hojoki Settings


You can change the default workspace here. Simply put the url for your workspace into the box above and hit save! :D

The url, which should look something like: http://my.hojoki.com/#/12345, will then be awesomatically clipped and cropped to ensure you always land on that workspace by default (and of course, your data stays between you and hojoki)

Just empty the box to return to the default setting of loading your principle Hojoki homepage.

Speedboat for Hojoki is open sourced under the GPLv3 and you can get all the source code from this .zip file.
